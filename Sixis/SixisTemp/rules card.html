How to Play

Sixis is a game about rolling smart and pressing your luck. Play it with your friends and family, or with our friendly robots — or both.

It's very easy to learn:

  1. When it's your turn, follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Take cards to score points.
  3. Flip cards to raise the stakes!

This game works best if you set the iPad down on a tabletop and have everyone sit around it. If you lack a tabletop, you can pass it around between turns instead.
